Bio

Hello! My name is Paradis. I always had a passion for art and the creative side of things, but always struggled with the actual creating part until early on in college. Once I took my first painting class, I felt a connection with it. Nowadays, I like to focus on digital art. I usually use an iPad mini which is nice for toting around with me versus a whole roll of paintbrushes and a bundle of Goldens. I also enjoy anything computer or tech-related, I love tinkering with things. I am a huge video game nerd and I enjoy a good scary story.
